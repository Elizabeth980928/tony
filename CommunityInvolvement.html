At MM SAFETY SOLUTIONS, we are passionate about the safety and wellness of the communities we are a part of and those we can reach. We have partnered with notably, 1Stop Health Foundation to host health and wellness campaigns where we provide optometry, audiology, dental and physiology services to men, women and children in need at the establishments and workplaces we serve. This initiative has been going on for about two years and it has been successful.

We assist with the provision of machinery and transportation services, ensuring that that health and safety protocols are maintained and followed and general staff support.

Our team of volunteers run clean up programs and safety controls to see that both the people and the environment are secure and protected at all times.

This initiative has proved to empower, not only the youth we work with on a voluntary basis but also the communities at large. Many kids are helped, most coming from disadvantaged backgrounds. They are given free eye care, free dental care, free therapy etc.

In the past two years we have engaged with atleast 8 schools, a municipal office and 2 local villages (broader project) and we can estimate to have reached out to over 300 people- and we are just getting started.
